A PROMISE WRITTEN IN BLOOD

Lay Upon The Earth

I will defend my brothers.
I will defend the weak.
I will sacrifice so that others may live.
I will pursue relentlessly all who threaten my people.
If you strike me or my brethren, you will lay upon the earth until you are buried in it.
THE BRIGADE'S OATH

This oath binds the soldiers of the Brigade—those who fight not for glory, but for the ones beside them.

In Lay Upon The Earth, Commander Geo Hart carries this promise into the void, where choices define survival and sacrifice becomes salvation.

"Written in 2016, this mantra was etched into our ribs—a permanent reminder that some bonds transcend blood, and some promises outlast the flesh that carries them."
— Amaro & Matthew

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When will Book One be available?

Book One: Lay Upon The Earth is currently available for pre-order and will release in late 2026. Sign up for the newsletter to get exact release date announcements.

What's the release schedule for the trilogy?

Book One releases in 2026, Book Two is planned for late 2026/early 2027, and Book Three is scheduled for 2027. Updates will be shared via newsletter and social media.

Will there be physical copies?

Yes! Each book will be available in hardcover, paperback, and digital formats. Special editions with exclusive content are also being planned.

Can I request the authors for an event or signing?

Absolutely! Use the contact form above with the "Event/Signing Request" subject. We'll do our best to accommodate requests based on location and availability.

Is this suitable for young adult readers?

The trilogy is written for adult audiences but can be enjoyed by mature teen readers. It contains complex themes, philosophical questions, and some intense action sequences.

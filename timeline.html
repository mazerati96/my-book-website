THE TIMELINE

Ten years of war. One impossible choice.

YEAR -8 BEFORE THE BRIGADE

THE WAR BEGINS

WAR EVENT
Ten Years Before Book One

Rezha erupts in conflict. What began as a planetary border dispute between empire factions escalates into full-scale war. Millions of soldiers are conscripted into the Hegemony back on Earth as Rezha falls. Among them: the soldier who will some day pilot the Brigade towards destiny.

IMPACT: 8 years of bloodshed begins
YEAR -2

THE SIGNAL DISCOVERED

CLASSIFIED
Two Years Before Book One

The Array detects a 36 Hertz frequency emanating from the supermassive black hole Charybdis Prime. Hegemony and Colonial Authority scientists can't explain it. It predates stellar formation. It shouldn't exist. Yet it hums, constant and eternal, like a prayer against entropy.

⚠️ Factions begin secret operations based on their interpretation of the signal and its possible meaning.

IMPACT: The race to the black hole begins, long before the Brigade is even a thought.
YEAR -1

THE DESERTION

BRIGADE
One Year Before Book One

Commander Geo witnesses the Hegemony's evil. An attempt at the lives of the farmers on Sozuna. An entire civilian population destroyed or evacuated as "collateral damage." Unable to reconcile duty with conscience, he makes an impossible choice: he deserts. His squad, Tahani, Ciel, Scout, and Cinder follow him into the dark.

IMPACT: The Brigade is born
YEAR 0

THE SACRIFICE

ANDROID
One Year Before Book One

After many failed attempts at building an android leader, the Brigade and Commander Geo make an impossible choice: sacrificing human memories to provide a template for the artificial consciousness to be. Something that could be lost forever. Geo chooses the insane: sacrificing ten years of memories to build the android.

The android is built.

IMPACT: The memories that will build the android
YEAR 0

SHE WAKES

BOOK ONE BEGINS
Present Day

The android opens her single optical sensor. She has circuits, titanium bones, borrowed memories, and no name. The Brigade needs a leader. Three empire fleets need her technology. And the signal from the black hole needs something no one understands yet.

This is where the story begins.

THE TIMELINE

Ten years of war. One impossible choice.

YEAR -10

THE WAR BEGINS

WAR EVENT
Ten Years Before Book One

Sector 2-B erupts in conflict. What began as a border dispute between empire factions escalates into full-scale war. Millions of soldiers are conscripted. Among them: the soldier whose memories will one day inhabit an android body.

IMPACT: 10 years of bloodshed begins
YEAR -8

THE FIRST DESERTION

BRIGADE
Eight Years Before Book One

Commander Reyes witnesses the Sector 2-B massacre—an entire civilian population destroyed as "collateral damage." Unable to reconcile duty with conscience, he makes an impossible choice: he deserts. Three of his squad follow him into the dark.

IMPACT: The Brigade is born
YEAR -6

THE BRIGADE GROWS

BRIGADE
Six Years Before Book One

More soldiers desert. Engineers. Medics. Pilots. People who chose conscience over empire. The Brigade becomes a ragtag fleet—hunted, desperate, but alive. They survive by salvaging tech, stealing supplies, and staying one step ahead of empire fleets.

IMPACT: 50+ deserters form a mobile fleet
YEAR -4

DR. CHEN'S ARRIVAL

CLASSIFIED
Four Years Before Book One

Dr. Kira Chen defects from empire research, bringing classified android technology. She claims ethical violations drove her out. But her obsession with consciousness transfer suggests deeper motives. She's been hearing the 36 Hz frequency for months.

IMPACT: Android creation becomes possible
YEAR -2

THE SIGNAL DISCOVERED

CLASSIFIED
Two Years Before Book One

The Array detects a 36 Hz frequency emanating from the supermassive black hole. Empire scientists can't explain it. It predates stellar formation. It shouldn't exist. Yet it hums, constant and eternal, like a prayer against entropy.

⚠️ Three empire factions immediately begin converging on the Array.

IMPACT: The race to the black hole begins
YEAR -1

THE SACRIFICE

ANDROID
One Year Before Book One

During a desperate battle, a Brigade soldier makes the ultimate sacrifice to save the fleet. But before they die, Dr. Chen makes an impossible offer: transfer their consciousness. Let their memories live on. Let them lead, even in death.

The soldier says yes.

IMPACT: The memories that will build the android
YEAR 0

SHE WAKES

BOOK ONE BEGINS
Present Day

The android opens her single optical sensor. She has circuits, titanium bones, borrowed memories, and no name. The Brigade needs a leader. Three empire fleets need her technology. And the signal from the black hole needs something no one understands yet.

This is where the story begins.
